RE:     INJUNCTION ORDER RELATING TO THE REDACTED PARTS OF THE COMMISSION OF INQUIRY REPORT

 

We refer to the publication of the Report of the Commission of Inquiry for the Turks and Caicos Islands 2008-2009 and the later Statement of His Excellency the Governor concerning its removal from the Commission website.

 

An order by way of injunction was granted at 9.00pm on Saturday July 18th, 2009 preventing any publication in legible or audible form of the redacted parts of the Report. The injunction order continues until 5.00pm on Monday July 20th, 2009, or sooner order. The matter will come before Chief Justice Gordon Ward at 10.00am on Monday July 20th, 2009 at the Court House, Providenciales.

 

We ask that you please read the attached order carefully and comply with its terms. We also enclose an electronic copy of an originating summons, application for an injunction and an affidavit in support. These later documents are not intended for publication and all matters can be raised in Court on July 20th, 2009 at 10.00am.
